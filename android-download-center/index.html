Installation Help

Here's a few extra tips you need in setting up our app. These are necessary as this app is not available in the Google Play Store. (Why you ask? Find out here)

Also if you get an 'install blocked' message while trying to install, just follow the instructions below.

Step 1

Download the Sportsbet app by tapping the 'Download' button above or by tapping here

Step 2

Go to 'Settings'

Step 3

Find 'Security'

Step 4

Scroll to 'Unknown Sources' and make sure that the box has a tick. Then close settings.

Step 5

Open 'Downloads' from your top bar or view your 'Downloads' folder.

Step 6

Tap the 'Sportsbet.apk' and the app will install automatically.

Why can't I find the Sportsbet Android app in the Google Play Store?
